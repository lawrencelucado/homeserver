# Refund, Exchange, Shipping, and Subscription Policy (Template)

1) Overview
- Brand: AromaCo
- Contact: support@yourdomain.com | RMA portal: yourdomain.com/returns
- Applies to: DTC retail orders (US unless stated). Wholesale terms see §8.

2) Refunds (DTC Retail)
- Window: 30 days from delivery (proof required) for sealed/unused items.
- Eligible: Sealed/unused; Defective/damaged on arrival; Wrong item received.
- Not eligible: Opened/used fragrances; Final‑sale/limited drops (if marked); Gift cards.
- Process: Submit RMA → receive approval + shipping label (if applicable) → return arrives → inspection → refund initiated.
- Costs: Original shipping not refundable. Return shipping deducted unless defective/our error.
- Timeline: Refund to original payment method within 5–10 business days post-inspection.
- Restocking: Up to 10% for non-defective returns (optional; remove if not used).

3) Exchanges
- Defective/damaged: Free exchange within 30 days; provide photos and lot code.
- Scent swap: One‑time courtesy exchange allowed within 14 days if unopened (customer pays return shipping) — optional.

4) Damages/Defects
- Report within 7 days of delivery with photos of product, packaging, and shipping label.
- Resolution: Replacement, store credit, or refund at our discretion.

5) Shipping Policy (Summary)
- Processing time: 1–2 business days (Mon–Fri, excluding holidays).
- Transit estimates: Domestic 2–7 days; International 7–21 days (DDP/DDU varies).
- Address issues: Customer responsible for incorrect/insufficient addresses.
- Lost packages: If courier marks delivered, file claim; we assist but cannot guarantee replacement.

6) Subscription Policy
- Plans: Monthly/Quarterly; billed at sign‑up then on the same day each cycle.
- Manage: Pause/skip/cancel anytime before next charge cutoff (48 hours prior) via account portal.
- Changes: Update scent preferences/address before cutoff; changes after cutoff apply next cycle.
- Cancellations: Effective next cycle; shipped boxes are non‑refundable unless defective.
- Pricing: Subject to change with 14‑day notice; active subscribers may receive legacy pricing at our discretion.
- Failed payments: Automatic retries over 7 days; after final failure, subscription pauses.
- Gifts/prepaid: Non‑refundable after first shipment; can be transferred (contact support).

7) International & Customs
- Duties/taxes: Shown at checkout (DDP) or due at delivery (DDU) depending on destination; customer responsible for DDU.
- Prohibited regions: List countries we do not ship to due to regulatory constraints.

8) Wholesale/Corporate Orders
- MOQ, lead times, and custom branding governed by separate wholesale terms.
- Defects/shortage claims within 5 business days of receipt with evidence.
- Returns require prior written authorization; custom/branded products are non‑returnable.

9) How to Start a Return
- Step 1: Visit RMA portal and enter order number + email.
- Step 2: Select reason and upload photos (if damaged/defective).
- Step 3: Receive RMA + label (if applicable). Pack items securely; include packing slip.
- Step 4: Track return; refund or exchange processed after inspection.
